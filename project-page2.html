The House

In this video, I want to show the game's mechanics and atmosphere. This includes how the main character moves, their animations, interactions with objects, having an inventory, and the enemy's artificial intelligence. The enemy uses their eyesight to find the main character, making their heart beat faster when spotted. They can also feel when the player is looking at them, which makes them a tough opponent. The location is designed so the main character can try to take shortcuts to escape the enemy. The enemy's artificial intelligence also walks around the maze in a set order. But if they lose sight of the player, they go to the nearest point and start patrolling from there. After the first level with mazes, there's another level where the player can hide in different places, and the enemy is alerted by the noise the main character makes when running. You can try this level on my github page.
